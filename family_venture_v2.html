The 7-Agent Family Venture System

Answer 7 questions. Six specialists analyze, debate, and disagree. One Narrator synthesizes the truth. Your personalized venture plan — built from your exact words.

Seven questions. Seven minutes. One plan.

Specific answers produce specific plans. The more honest you are, the sharper the output.

CLIENT
Who is this plan for?
Your name or family name — appears on the cover of your plan.
QUESTION 01
Describe your household in one sentence: Who lives under your roof, what are 2–3 core family values, and what stage of life are you in?
Be specific about people, values, and stage — this shapes everything that follows.
QUESTION 02
What's the ONE biggest challenge or stressor in your family life or finances right now — and how long has it been an issue?
Name the actual number or situation. Duration matters — how long has this been unresolved?
QUESTION 03
In 3–5 years, where do you want your family to be — one specific financial milestone and one life/legacy change?
Not a range. A specific number. And a concrete legacy change — not a feeling.
QUESTION 04
In the last 6–12 months: Where does most of your family time, energy, and money actually go — and where do you wish it went to build toward a family venture?
The honest answer, not the ideal. The gap between these two is where the plan lives.
QUESTION 05
On a scale of 1–10, how confident are you that your family could successfully launch and run a joint venture — and what one thing would raise that by 3+ points?
Give the number first. Then explain what's driving it and what would change it.
QUESTION 06
If we delivered a tailored family venture plan today, what's the single first action your family would realistically take — and what might stop you?
Be honest about the stopper. "Time" is not a real answer — what's the real blocker?
QUESTION 07 NEW
What does your family know how to do better than most people — a skill, industry knowledge, or connection that others would genuinely pay for?
This is your unfair advantage. The venture ideas built for you come directly from this answer.
